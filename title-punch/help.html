Alpha Channel Information

Title Punch is designed to enhance media that contains transparency information in the Alpha channel. Titles and transparent media (PNG images, keyed clips) are ideal candidates for the effects. You can verify alpha channel presence through FCPX's View menu and Alpha channel option.

Before and after Title Punch

Title Background Object

Motion's Title Background Object can interfere with Title Punch effects by pulling in the alpha information from underneath it. The solution is to disable or remove background clips when using titles with Title Background objects.

Applying Templates

Two approaches for applying templates:

  1. Select a clip or edit point, then double-click the desired effect, transition, or title.
  2. Drag templates directly from the browser to the timeline for clips, edits, or blank spots.

Drawing Titles

Eraser, Oops, Redact, and Underline are Titles that get placed on top of other Titles in order to draw over them. Each offers multiple styles and built-in animation for shape drawing.

Eraser Workaround

The Eraser's hole-punching behavior can cause problems with background clips. The workaround: make a compound clip with just the two titles — do not include the background clip(s).
